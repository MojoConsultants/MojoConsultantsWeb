Service Agreement (Summary)

Plain‑English overview of our typical engagement terms. This page is informational only and not legal advice.

Scope

Scope, deliverables, and milestones are defined in your project proposal or statement of work (SOW). Any additions or changes are handled via a written change request.

Timeline

We provide a project schedule with key dates for reviews and approvals. Delays in feedback may shift the schedule.

Fees & Payments

  • Fixed‑price or milestone‑based billing as specified in your SOW.
  • 50% deposit to begin, balance due at launch (unless otherwise noted).
  • Invoices are due within 14 days.

Client Responsibilities

  • Provide timely access to assets, accounts, and stakeholders.
  • Review and approve deliverables within agreed windows.

Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, you own the final deliverables for your project. We may reference non‑confidential work samples in our portfolio.

Confidentiality

We keep your information confidential and use it solely to perform the agreed services.

Warranties & Limitations

Work is provided “as‑is” to the extent permitted by law. We are not liable for indirect or consequential damages.

Cancellation

Either party may terminate with written notice. Fees for completed work and in‑progress milestones remain payable.
