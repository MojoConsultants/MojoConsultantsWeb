Executive Summary

Mojo Park's security program focuses on Zero Trust, least privilege, continuous monitoring, and incident readiness. It aligns policy, controls, and automation to protect resident data, payments, and platform uptime.

This page adapts the structure of the prior Manasa Park security outline for "Mojo Park".

Scope & Assets

  • SaaS platform (web, mobile, APIs)
  • Resident/tenant data & payments
  • Admin/partner portals
  • Cloud infrastructure & CI/CD
  • Cybersecurity
  • AI / ML

Top Risks

  • Account takeover & weak MFA coverage
  • Secrets exposure in CI/CD or repos
  • Third-party/vendor data sharing risk
  • Misconfigured cloud services
  • Cybersecurity
  • AI / ML

90-Day Roadmap

  1. Weeks 1-2: Asset inventory, baseline policies, MFA/SSO rollout.
  2. Weeks 3-4: Centralized logging (SIEM), alert tuning, runbook drafts.
  3. Weeks 5-6: Vulnerability scans, patch cadence, secrets scanning.
  4. Weeks 7-8: Backups & DR tests; RTO/RPO targets.
  5. Weeks 9-10: Tabletop exercise, incident comms templates.
  6. Weeks 11-12: Audit prep (NIST/ISO), metrics review.
